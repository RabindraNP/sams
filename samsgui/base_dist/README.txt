      SAMS - Spectral Analysis and Management System
CSTARS - Center for Spatial Technologies and Remote Sensing
            University of California, Davis


The Spectral Analysis and Management System (SAMS) is used
to manage field spectra databases and analyses. Options are
included to import, organize, visualize, manipulate, and
export spectral data. For up-to-date information and new
releases, please visit: http://sams.casil.ucdavis.edu/

Please read the copyright agreement located under the
doc subdirectory.

For bug reporting, please use our tracker system in:
   http://casil.ucdavis.edu/tracker/?group_id=32


Enjoy!
